Marina Gerber is an experienced Office and Property Manager responsible for ensuring safe, compliant, and efficient workplaces that support business performance. With more than 25 years of experience spanning defence, corporate, and consulting environments, she specialises in operations management, facilities and property management, vendor and contract management, and risk and compliance oversight.

In her role at SSW, Marina oversees the day-to-day operation of the office and manages property-related responsibilities, with a strong focus on continuous improvement, clear communication, and practical delivery. Her previous experience in the pharmaceutical, technology, and logistics sectors, combined with more than a decade of service as a Naval Combat Officer, equipped her with the ability to remain composed under pressure, manage competing priorities, and support executive leadership with reliability and professionalism.

Marina focuses on building systems that work, supporting high-performing teams, and creating stability in fast-paced, changing environments. She believes that good organisation is not just about maintaining order — it is about creating the conditions for people to do their best work.
